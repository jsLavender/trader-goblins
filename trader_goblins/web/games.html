🎮 Games

A little home arcade — free, ad-free, runs entirely in your browser. Progress saves on this device.

🐝

Spelling Bee

Make as many words as you can from seven letters — every word uses the center letter. Find the pangram. A fresh puzzle whenever you want one.
🟩

Wordle

Guess the five-letter word in six tries. Green is right spot, yellow is wrong spot. Play as many as you like.
🔗

Connections

Sort sixteen words into four secret groups of four. Watch out for the overlaps that fit more than one group.
✏️

Crossword

Three homemade mini crosswords to start. You can also open any .puz / .ipuz file from the player.

Mini #1 Mini #2 Mini #3 Open a file…
🧊

Mech-E-Mech

A command-not-twitch mech roguelite. Steer a slow fortress through a branching run while your turrets auto-battle the swarm.

All games are open-source clones (their own word lists / original puzzles), self-hosted here. Built with the Spelling Bee by ConorSheehan1 (MIT), react-wordle (MIT), react-connections-game (GPL-3.0), and the Crossword Nexus player (BSD-3). The crosswords here are original.
