🧊 Mech-E-Mech

A command-not-twitch mech roguelite. Steer a slow fortress through a branching run; your turrets auto-battle the swarm.

Hold / drag to move · Space (or the button) fires missiles · 1 / 2 / 3 switch ammo · tap a lit map node to choose your route.
